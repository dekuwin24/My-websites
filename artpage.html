PRINCESS POUKOU

Princess Abla Pokou was the princess of the Ashanti
and the sister of Dakon, the King of the Ashnati of Ghanh in 17th century. At that period, war broke out after the death of the late King Ossei Tutu, her uncle.Later during the war Dakon died and left Pokou alone to take care of tribe safety. This is where the story of Abla Pukou starts as the savior, the mother and the first queen of the Baoule.
